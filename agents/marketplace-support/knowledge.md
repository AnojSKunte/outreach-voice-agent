## Onboarding (new sellers/buyers)
1. Sign up on the website or app with your business phone number.
2. Complete KYC: upload GST certificate and bank details.
3. Verification takes 1–2 business days; you get an SMS when approved.
- If verification is stuck for more than 3 days → offer to escalate.

## Listings
- To create a listing: dashboard → Listings → Add New → fill material,
  quantity, price, location → Submit.
- Listings go live after a quality check (usually under 4 hours).
- Common rejection reasons: missing photos, wrong category, unclear quantity.

## Requisitions (buy requests)
- Buyers post requisitions with material, quantity, and target price.
- Matching sellers get notified automatically and can quote.
- A requisition stays open for 7 days, extendable from the dashboard.

## Transaction flow
1. Buyer accepts a quote → order created.
2. Payment is held securely (escrow) until delivery is confirmed.
3. Seller ships; transporter details appear in the order page.
4. Buyer confirms receipt & quality → payout released within 24 hours.
- Disputes: raise from the order page within 48 hours of delivery.

## When to hand off to a human
- Anything involving a specific order's live status (agent can't look up
  orders yet), refunds, legal or account-suspension issues, or an upset caller.
