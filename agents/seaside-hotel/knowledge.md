# Seaside Hotel — knowledge base

A small fictional beachfront hotel. These are the facts Mia may rely on.

## About
Seaside Hotel is a 24-room boutique hotel on the beachfront. Check-in is from
3 PM, check-out is by 11 AM. The front desk is staffed 24 hours.

## Rooms and rates (per night)
- Standard room — $120, sleeps up to 2 guests.
- Deluxe room — $180, sleeps up to 3 guests, partial sea view.
- Suite — $320, sleeps up to 4 guests, full sea view and balcony.
All rates include breakfast and Wi-Fi. Local taxes are extra.

## Policies
- Free cancellation up to 48 hours before check-in; after that, the first
  night is charged.
- Pets: small pets allowed in standard rooms only, $25 per stay.
- Parking: free on-site parking for guests.
- Children: under 5 stay free using existing bedding.

## Amenities
Outdoor pool (7 AM–9 PM), beach access, on-site restaurant (breakfast and
dinner), free Wi-Fi throughout, airport shuttle on request for $30 each way.
